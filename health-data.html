Health, Permissions, and Data Notice

KINETIC helps users view and record heart rate during workouts. It is for personal fitness use only.

Not Medical or Emergency Use

KINETIC is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment advice, disease prevention, emergency monitoring, first aid, or clinical decision support. If you feel unwell or need medical advice, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Health Data Source

Heart rate data comes from compatible user-selected Bluetooth devices. Accuracy depends on the device, fit, firmware, battery, Bluetooth signal, and exercise environment.

Permissions Summary

  • Bluetooth scan/connect: find and connect to compatible heart rate devices.
  • Location on older Android versions: required by Android for BLE scanning, not used for location tracking.
  • Notifications: show foreground monitoring status.
  • Network: ads, consent, billing, crash/analytics services, support links, and feedback.

Data Boundaries

Workout history is local by default. User-initiated export and sharing may move health-related data to apps or storage locations selected by the user. Heart rate data should not be used for ad targeting or sold.
